MouseTrap+ is the first modern mousetrap that syncs to your smartphone. Running on a rechargeable lithium battery, MouseTrap+ uses a tiny sensor to detect micro-movements and heat signatures in the hard-to-find places of your home and will move itself into optimal position to bait and catch the mice. It's the roomba of pest control. Once it has a rodent in its humane snares, MouseTrap+ can send alerts through either SMS, e-mail or push notification, making it a worry free trap that you don't have to check every day.

Inside MouseTrap+, a speaker emits high-frequency noises inaudible to the human ear but enticing to rodents. Once a mouse enters the trap, the inner chamber compresses in micro-seconds for a speedy, painless kill. To empty the trap, hold MouseTrap+ over a waste basket and press the release button. If a quick and painless death is still too cruel, you can turn on MouseTrap+'s catch-and-release mode, which will simply trap the mouse in the chamber and allow you to free him in the nearest bucolic wonderland.

FAQ

How does MouseTrap+ tell mice from normal movement?
Highly calibrated sensors have been trained to detect only movement from mouse-size objects. Combined with an infrared sensor, MouseTrap+ is smart enough to know the difference between a gyrating appliance and a pesky rodent.
What if it runs low on power?
When it's time to recharge MouseTrap+, it will send you a notification asking you to charge it with the included charging cable.
What devices does MouseTrap+ support?
MouseTrap+ works best with Android and iOS phones, but SMS and e-mail notification options mean it will work with any phone that can send and receive text messages or e-mails.
